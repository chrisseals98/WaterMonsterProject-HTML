About the Axolotl Species Preservation Center

At the Axolotl Species Preservation Center, our passion is the preservation and conservation of one of the world's most extraordinary creatures—the axolotl. Our organization was founded with a clear mission: to protect and ensure the survival of this unique amphibian species, which is currently listed as critically endangered.

Our Vision

"We envision a world where axolotls flourish in their natural habitat, their ecosystems thrive, and their remarkable regenerative abilities continue to inspire and contribute to scientific advancements."

Michael Belfort, APC Founder

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of biologists, conservationists, researchers, and passionate individuals who share a deep commitment to the axolotl and its environment. Our expertise, combined with our love for these creatures, drives our conservation efforts.

What We Do

Habitat Preservation

We work tirelessly to protect and restore the natural habitats of axolotls, focusing on areas such as the Xochimilco canals, where these creatures are found.

Scientific Research

Our organization supports research projects aimed at understanding axolotls and their unique regenerative capabilities. This knowledge is vital for developing effective conservation strategies.

Conservation Initiatives

We collaborate with local communities, government bodies, and other organizations to implement conservation programs, raise awareness, and foster support for axolotl preservation.
